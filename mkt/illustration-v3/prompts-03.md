# 03 — REINVENTING THE ADDRESS WHEN THE BUILDING ALREADY HAS ONE

**Point 3: The internet already solved identity**
**Framework:** Structural Overkill

**The Logic:** Multiple teams are building new identity infrastructure from scratch — a skill marketplace with invented namespaces, a GitHub org/repo hierarchy as identity, an Ethereum smart contract registry — all to answer one question: "Is this really from who it claims to be?" Meanwhile, domains already answer that question. They're on every brand's business card, email signature, and invoice. DNS, TLS, DNSSEC, and Certificate Transparency already verify them. 350 million domains, 4.5 billion users, trillions in daily commerce — all running on infrastructure that's free to use.
