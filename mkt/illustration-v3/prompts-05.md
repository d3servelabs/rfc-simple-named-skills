# 05 — EVERY COMPANY WILL NEED ONE (AGAIN)

**Point 5: Every brand will need to publish skills**
**Framework:** Signal vs. Noise

**The Logic:** In the 2000s every company needed a website. In the 2010s every company needed a mobile app. In the 2020s every company will need AI-accessible skills. Each wave was obvious in retrospect, controversial at the time. The question each time is the same: will publishing be permissionless (like the web) or gated (like app stores)? DVS makes skill publishing as open as putting up a website.
