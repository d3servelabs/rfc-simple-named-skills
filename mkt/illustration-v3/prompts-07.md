# 07 — YARD SIGNS vs. CITY BLOCKS

**Point 7: Open standards beat proprietary conventions**
**Framework:** Structural Overkill

**The Logic:** Proprietary skill platforms are like temporary advertising — yard signs hammered into weedy lawns, billboards being hastily painted, airplanes dragging banners — ephemeral, controlled by one party, gone when they leave. Domain-based trust built on open standards is like a city with established buildings bearing polished company signs, standing on permanent cross-streets named after the infrastructure that holds everything up: DNSSEC, HTTPS, Certificate Authorities, DNS, TLS. The streets were there before any building and will be there after.
