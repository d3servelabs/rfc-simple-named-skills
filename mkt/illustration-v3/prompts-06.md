# 06 — THE GAP IS THE RISK

**Point 6: Autonomy accelerating faster than defenses**
**Framework:** Financial Malpractice

**The Logic:** Agent capabilities — MCP, A2A, tool use, computer use, multi-agent delegation, autonomous coding — are growing exponentially. Trust verification at the skill layer has remained at zero. The widening gap between capability and verification IS the risk. Every increment of autonomy without verification is an increment of unpriced liability.
