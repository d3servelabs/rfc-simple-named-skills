# 07 — YARD SIGNS vs. CITY BLOCKS

Create an animated SVG infographic contrasting temporary proprietary platforms vs permanent open infrastructure.

On one side: flimsy yard signs on a weedy lawn — "Trust My Skill Platform", "New Registry Coming Soon", a plane dragging a banner. Temporary. Controlled by one company. Gone when they leave.

On the other side: a gleaming city skyline. Established buildings bearing company signs (Stripe, Shopify, GitHub, Cloudflare), standing on permanent streets named after the infrastructure: DNSSEC Avenue, HTTPS Boulevard, DNS Street, TLS Drive. The streets were there before any building and will be there after.

Open standards outlast any single platform.
