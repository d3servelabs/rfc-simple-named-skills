# 05 — EVERY COMPANY WILL NEED ONE (AGAIN)

Create an animated SVG infographic showing a timeline of three technology waves.

2000s: Every company needed a website. Publishing was permissionless — buy a domain, upload HTML.
2010s: Every company needed a mobile app. Publishing was gated — app store approval required.
2020s: Every company needs AI skills. The question: will it be gated (marketplace approval, listing fees) or permissionless (publish on your domain, instant, free)?

Show this as a clear timeline with a fork at the end: one path leads to the gated dead end, the other to the permissionless open road. DVS makes skill publishing as open as putting up a website.
