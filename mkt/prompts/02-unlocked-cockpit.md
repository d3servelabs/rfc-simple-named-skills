# 02 — LEAVING THE COCKPIT DOOR UNLOCKED BECAUSE THE PLANE IS FANCY

**Point 1: Agents are dangerously powerful**
**Framework:** Structural Overkill

**The Logic:** The more powerful agents become (code execution, multi-step workflows, autonomous coding), the higher the stakes when they execute something they shouldn't. Power without trust verification is liability.
