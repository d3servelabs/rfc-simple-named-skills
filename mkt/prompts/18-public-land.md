# 18 — PAYING RENT TO STAND ON PUBLIC LAND

**Point 7: Open standards beat proprietary ecosystems**
**Framework:** Financial Malpractice

**The Logic:** Proprietary platform SDKs lock publishers into ecosystems with licensing costs and vendor dependencies. An IETF RFC is public, free, and vendor-neutral — any platform can implement it.
