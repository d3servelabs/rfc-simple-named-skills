# SVG Animation Prompts for DVS Article

20 animated SVGs. Each analogy is rooted in an everyday experience people immediately recognize. Designed for Gemini 3.1 Pro to generate self-contained animated SVGs with CSS/SMIL.

All: 800x418 (viewBox="0 0 800 418"), loop infinitely.

---

### 1. "Handing Over All Your Keys"
**Style: Ligne claire (Tintin/Hergé) — uniform line weight, flat bold colors, no gradients, Franco-Belgian comic aesthetic.**

You meet someone for the first time. They seem helpful. So you hand them your house key, car key, office badge, phone passcode, and credit card — all at once, on a single keyring. That's what installing an unverified skill is. Animated: a friendly-looking character holds out their hand. A person drops key after key onto it — house, car, office, safe, phone — each with a label. The character smiles wider with each key. Final frame: the character walks away with everything. The person stands empty-handed.

### 2. "The Fake Delivery Uniform"
